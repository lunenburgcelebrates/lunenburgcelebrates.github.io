June 7 – 11, 2017

It’s Lunenburg’s birthday & we’re going to celebrate in style!

Preparations are underway for our 3rd annual BE A TOURIST IN YOUR TOWN weekend! Once again various groups and organizations are hosting events to help mark the Town’s 264th birthday. The actual birthday is Wednesday, June 7 but most activities will take place the following weekend.

The Be a Tourist concept was designed to help encourage residents and visitors alike to take some time to explore all that our beautiful town has to offer and enjoy the assortment of activities that take place in our own backyard.

Full events schedule to be posted soon!
