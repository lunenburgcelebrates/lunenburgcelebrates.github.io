Screening - I Am Not A Rock Star

Lunenburg Academy of Music Performance, 97 Kaulbach Street. Friday, June 5 at 7:30 PM. LAMP is proud to present a screening of the award-winning, full-length feature documentary I Am Not A Rock Star, about Canadian classical pianist Marika Bournaki. Ms Bournaki is at LAMP this month as part of a four-week residency and will be on hand for the screening. Shot in cinema verite style in concert halls, hotels and airports around the world and inspired by stunning piano performances, the film follows Marika’s career between the ages of 12 and 20 and dramatically unfolds to reveal the coming of age of an ambitious, talented pianist. Admission $5

Folk Harbour Festival – Lennie Gallant at The Pearl Theatre

The Lunenburg Folk Harbour Society is pleased to present Mr. Lenny Gallant at the Pearl Theatre, Lunenburg on June 6 at 7:00p.m. The Rustico PEI native is a renowned bilingual singer-songwriter with ten albums to his credit including two in French.

Awards and nominations for Lennie Gallant are legion including JUNOs, East Coast Music Awards and Les Prix Eloizes. He was recently honored with the 2014 Canadian Folk Music Award for Artist of the Year for his latest recording “Live Acoustic at The Carleton”. People love his rich storytelling, instant connection with the audience and the emotions poured into his music and lyrics.

For ticket information: www.folkharbour.com

Blue Dream Project

Doors open from 1-5pm on Saturday at the Big Boat Shed at the end of Montague Street. Blue Dream Project is an Italo-Canadian non-profit organization, established to help preserve and protect the marine environment from pollution caused by plastic.

"Blue Dream Project is building a 60' schooner, built entirely out of wood, in the traditional style and methods of Nova Scotia. The new ambassador will be made available to citizens, researchers and students that want to participate in analysis and information campaigns about pollution of the sea and raise public awareness on the health of our oceans."
